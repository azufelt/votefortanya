Development

Elk Ridge is growing at a much faster pace than the city anticipated.

  • Establish a vision for the future of our beloved city.
  • Work with developers to maintain City vision.
  • Ensure developers follow city growth-plan.
  • Enforce fair compensation for developer concessions.

I chose to make Elk Ridge my home because I wanted to raise my kids in a community that works hard, serves others, and has good values.

Budget

Items in the capital facilities plan need to funded to support future endeavors.

  • Make sure that contracts are being properly bid.

  • Make sure spending reflects the best interests of the citizens of Elk Ridge.
